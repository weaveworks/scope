Cello is a map of your network, and a lot more.
No configuration, nothing out-of-date—just the facts.

Topology graphs.

Just the facts about your machines and traffic.

Devs empowered.

A real-time view.

Cello shows you exactly what's happening on your network, right now. It can show you an overview of the complete topology, or focus on specific segments. Cello helps you verify correct operations, and find troubled applications.

Group hosts.

You can classify the traffic and servers based on patterns. Similar servers can be grouped and colored based on their behavior. The picture is always accurate and up-to-date—no need to edit config files.

Drill down.

See network statistics per host, or inspect communication between individual interfaces. Use filters to look only at certain servers or traffic. All views are bookmarkable and simple to share.
